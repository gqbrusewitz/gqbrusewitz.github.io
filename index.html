    Weekly Training Flow

    Keep intensity balanced with recovery.

    • Mon: Upper push (bench/overhead) + core
    • Tue: Lower (squat/hinge) + mobility
    • Wed: Conditioning (20–30 min zone 2)
    • Thu: Upper pull (rows/pull-ups) + arms
    • Fri: Lower power + carries
    • Sat: Optional finisher or sport
    • Sun: Full rest, light walk, or stretching

    Swap days as needed—just keep two rest/recovery slots per week.

    Cardio & Recovery

    Plug these in around your lifting days.

    • Zone 2: 25–40 min easy bike, jog, or row.
    • Intervals: 8–12 rounds of 30s on / 90s off.
    • Steps: 7–10k daily as a steady baseline.
    • Mobility: 8–10 min hips/shoulders after lifting.
    • Sleep: 7–9 hrs; short naps if training volume rises.
    • Hydration: 16–20 oz water with electrolytes pre-lift.
